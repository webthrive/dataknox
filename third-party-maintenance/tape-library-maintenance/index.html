Why Tape Library TPM

Tape Isn't Dead. OEM Tape Support Pricing Is.

Tape libraries remain the most cost-effective long-term storage medium for archival, backup, and compliance. OEM maintenance contracts for aging tape libraries are disproportionately expensive — particularly for StorageTek and IBM TS systems past their declared EoL. Dataknox provides post-warranty and post-EoL tape library maintenance at 30–75% less, with parts sourced from a deep tape library component inventory covering LTO drives, robotic assemblies, and library chassis.
