End-of-Life Support FAQs

End-of-life (EoL) hardware support is third-party maintenance coverage for equipment that the OEM has officially declared past its support window. Dataknox provides repair, parts, and on-site response for EoL hardware across servers, storage, and networking — up to 10 years past the OEM's EoL date.
Dataknox can support hardware up to 10 years past the OEM's declared end-of-life date. Parts availability and specific SLA options vary by platform — we'll confirm coverage during the quoting process.
For hardware maintenance, yes. Dataknox delivers the same on-site response times and parts coverage as OEM contracts. The difference is that OEM contracts often bundle software entitlements and feature upgrades that post-EoL environments no longer need or receive.
Post-warranty support covers hardware after the OEM warranty expires but before the EoL declaration. EoL support covers hardware after the OEM has fully ended its maintenance offerings. Dataknox covers both scenarios under a single contract.
