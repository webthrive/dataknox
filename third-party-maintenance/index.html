What Is Third-Party Maintenance?

Third-party maintenance (TPM) is hardware support delivered by an independent service provider — not the original equipment manufacturer. When your Cisco SmartNet, HPE Foundation Care, or Dell ProSupport contract expires, TPM is the alternative that maintains the same service quality at a fraction of the cost.

OEM support costs increase 8–15% annually and often become unavailable entirely once hardware reaches official end-of-life (EoL) or end-of-service-life (EoSL) status. Dataknox bridges that gap — supporting your infrastructure for up to 10 years past EoL, on your schedule, not the OEM's.

For enterprise IT teams managing hundreds of devices across multiple OEM ecosystems, TPM also solves the multi-vendor fragmentation problem: one Dataknox co-terminous contract replaces separate Cisco, Dell, HPE, and NetApp agreements.

  • Replaces OEM support contracts at 30–85% lower cost
  • Supports hardware past official EoL and EoSL dates
  • Co-terminous agreements consolidate multi-OEM environments
  • Domestic-based expert engineers on first call — no tiered triage
  • All parts fully tested with 1-year replacement warranty
  • SAE AS5553 compliant parts sourcing — counterfeit avoidance
